Web Host Research

By: Zane Melzer

I chose 3 different web hosting services. I explain what is good about each. AS well as choosing my favorite and telling you why.

Note: The prices shown are for a '.com' domain with the name jeffspartyworld.com.

hostgator

Cost: $2.71 /mo

Features:

  • Free domain for first year
  • 24/7 technical support
  • free advertising credit
  • unmetered disk sopace and bandwidth

inmotion

Cost: $2.49 /mo

Features:

  • on click installs
  • unlimited bandwidth
  • money back guarentee for 90 days
  • 24/7 support

liquidweb

Cost: $9.50 /mo

Features:

  • better speed, better security, better suport
  • full server access
  • automatic plugin and core updates
  • no pageview/ traffic limits

My Choice: hostgator.com

Although Hostgator may not have the highest tech. It has the best monthly price and has a great 45 day money back guarentee. this site also also has 24/7 customer service. As well as a free domain for the first year
